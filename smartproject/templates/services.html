Our Services

IoT Safety Device

The heart of Suraksha is our IoT device—a sleek, portable gadget with buttons, a speaker/recorder, and blue LED border lights that activate on startup. Designed for emergencies, it’s your instant connection to safety, triggered with a single press or voice command.

SMS Alerts

With a single click on the Suraksha device, send your precise location via SMS to registered contacts—parents, friends, or anyone you trust. It’s fast, reliable, and ensures help is just a message away.

Phone Call Notifications

Opt for an automated phone call alert with a pre-recorded message and your location details. This service ensures your emergency reaches your contacts even if they miss a text, providing an audible call to action.

Email Alerts

Receive detailed email notifications with your location and, if recorded, your voice message. Perfect for contacts who prefer a digital record or need to coordinate help from a distance.

WhatsApp Messages

Send instant WhatsApp messages with your location and emergency details to your registered contacts. This widely-used platform ensures quick communication in a familiar format.

Voice Assistance

Activate the voice assistant with a custom phrase like "Activate Suraksha" to control the device hands-free. Issue commands, get navigation help, or trigger alerts—all with your voice, making safety effortless in high-stress moments.

Drone Safety Support

Our cutting-edge drone service (in development) deploys an autonomous drone to your location upon an alert. It surveys the scene, relays live data to authorities, and ensures rapid response, bypassing obstacles like traffic.

Alerts to Police & Help Centers

Long press the button and use a custom keyword (e.g., "help me") to send your location and recorded message to nearby police stations, health centers, or fire services. This service ensures professional help arrives when you need it most.

Device Customization

Tailor your Suraksha device via our website—select design templates, set communication preferences (SMS, email, etc.), and customize voice assistant settings. Make it yours, from aesthetics to functionality.
